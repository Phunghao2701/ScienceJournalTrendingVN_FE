# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Researchers, students, journal analysts, and academic administrators who need to explore Vietnamese scientific publication trends quickly. They arrive with an information task: find relevant articles, compare journals or topics, inspect authors and institutions, and export or save useful result sets.

## Product Purpose

Trending VN is the primary product surface for discovering and analyzing publication trends in Vietnam. It turns article, journal, author, topic, access-status, and year-distribution data into a searchable research workspace. Success means users can move from a broad trend question to a trustworthy article set, understand why the results matter, and continue their work through detail pages, saved queries, sharing, or export.

## Brand Personality

Professional, trustworthy, data-focused. The interface should feel like a serious research instrument: calm enough for long sessions, structured enough for dense evidence, and polished without becoming decorative.

## Anti-references

Do not let the old cloned project context overpower the Trending VN redesign. Avoid flashy SaaS landing-page patterns, AI-looking gradient-heavy surfaces, excessive rounded cards, ornamental motion, decorative glassmorphism, and marketing-style hero layouts. Avoid burying scientific data behind oversized visuals or vague copy.

## Design Principles

1. Keep trend exploration task-first: search, filters, analytics, article lists, and result actions should stay visible, predictable, and fast.
2. Preserve research credibility through restrained visual choices, readable density, clear hierarchy, and consistent data labels.
3. Make filters and pagination predictable: query changes should reset pagination appropriately and active filters should always be legible.
4. Treat every data state as part of the product: loading, empty, error, and successful states should be clear and useful.
5. Reuse the established ResearchPulse visual vocabulary where it supports Trending VN, but prioritize the remade trending design when old cloned surfaces conflict.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target readable, keyboard-friendly product UI with strong text contrast, visible focus states, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion alternatives for non-essential animation. Scientific notation, article titles, and dense metadata must remain readable across desktop and mobile viewports.
